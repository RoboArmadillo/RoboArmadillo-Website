About Us!

My name is Adam Ferguson. I have been a student doing robotics for the past 5 years. I've just finished my final year at school in Britain studying the International Baccalaureate Diploma Program. For my extended essay, a 4000 word research project as part of my IB I chose to make a Raspberry Pi and Arduino based Robotics kit. I was aiming it at 13-15 years olds in order to get younger teenagers into robotics and electronics in addition to programming. It is however a very diverse kit with what I hope is a highly intuative programming method that anyone can pick up in an afternoon. All of my work is open-source and based upon open-source hardware therefore in time you will be able to find my system diagrams online and all of my software is available to download on Github. I hope you enjoy the kit as much as I do, Happy Hacking!
